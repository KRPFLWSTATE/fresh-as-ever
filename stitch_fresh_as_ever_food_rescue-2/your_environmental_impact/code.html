Our Methodology

How we calculate CO2e

We use a standardized conversion factor where 1 kg of rescued food averts approximately 2.5 kg of CO2 equivalents (CO2e). This accounts for the emissions saved from preventing food waste in landfills and the energy conserved from agricultural production, transportation, and preparation.

Bag Weight Estimation

For simplicity, we estimate that each 'Rescue Bag' contains an average of 1 kg of food. Actual weights may vary depending on the merchant and the specific items rescued.
